About Cash Pot

Cash Pot is Jamaica’s most exciting daily lottery game, inspired by the classic Drop Pan. It’s simple, fun, and packed with chances to win big—six times a day, every day.

With just one lucky number between 1 and 36, you could turn a small bet into a big payout. The odds of winning are 36 to 1, and if you do, you’ll score $280 for every $10 wagered—that’s 28 times your bet. 

Cash Pot draws happen six times daily at:
8:30 AM | 10:30 AM | 1:00 PM | 3:00 PM | 5:00 PM | 8:25 PM
(Every day except Christmas Day and Good Friday)

You can catch the live draws on Television Jamaica, HITZ 92FM, and IRIE FM.

How To Play

  • Choose Your Lucky Number(s):

    Select your favourite number(s) from a range of 1 to 36. Your chosen numbers are your ticket to potential winnings.

  • Place Your Bet

    Start your Cash Pot journey with a minimum bet of just $10 and a maximum of $500. You have the flexibility to place wagers on as many numbers within the 1 to 36 range as you like, and you can do so as often as you wish per draw

  • Win Big

    If luck is on your side and your chosen number matches the one randomly selected during the live draw, you'll win $280 for every $10 wagered. That's a fantastic 28 times the amount you bet, offering you the chance for an incredible win.
